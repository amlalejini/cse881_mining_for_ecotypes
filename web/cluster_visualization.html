Visualization: Mining for Ecotypes (Avida Data)

About

The Big Picture

We used clustered populations of digital organisms based on genotypic and phenotypic similarity between organisms in the population. We used these clusters to detect co-existing ecotypes within the population and to infer phylogenetic relationships between clusters at one point in time to clusters at a past point in time.

The Data

This data was collected using the Avida Digital Evolution Platform. We replicated the environment described here.
